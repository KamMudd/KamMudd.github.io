GEAR UP 4 THE SUMMER!

Swedish artist Mio Linzie designed the rainbow jerseys. The crest of the jersey features swords and a compass rose. The swords represent the story of "wandering and coming home in Vegas." The compass rose represents the city, nature, hockey and the queer community coming together in a golden light.

The jerseys are set to be auctioned off with the proceeds benefiting Henderson Equality Center, a local center that supports at-risk LGBTQ youths and adults.
